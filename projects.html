Bark Rangers

I started my developing journey by customizing a Shopify site for my crafting hobby, Bark Rangers! I utilized one of their commercial off the shelf products for the backend and customized the front-end portion. I created captivating full height heroes to convey a lifestyle brand, functional tabs to display product information, a compelling photo grid of customer reviews, a user-friendly FAQ page, and even fixed all of the responsive issues from the original code!

Shopify | Liquid | HTML | CSS | Javascript

Bark Dashboard

To further practice my coding, I built an interactive dashboard that visualizes data I collect from my shop. The dashboard consists of sections with sales goals, revenue and percent-to-goal data by each month and year! I utilize graphs that show popular product categories, popular sizes and colors, site traffic sources, and an interactive map to show where dog owners are, including the dog bandanna sizes in each area. Because this contains sensitive data from my shop, I do not have this site published online. However, I’m happy to showcase it through a screen share meeting!

HTML | CSS | Javascript | Bootstrap | Plotly | Leaflet | SQL
